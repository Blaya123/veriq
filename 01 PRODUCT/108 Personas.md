# User Personas

## Purpose
Represent the key user segments VERIQ serves.

---

## Persona 1: Freelancer Sarah

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Age** | 28 |
| **Role** | Freelance Graphic Designer |
| **Location** | Lagos, Nigeria |
| **Tech Level** | Comfortable with apps, not technical |
| **Income** | $2,000 — $4,000 / month |

**Behavior:**
- Manages clients via WhatsApp, email, and Instagram DMs
- Uses Google Calendar for scheduling
- Sends invoices manually via PDF
- Tracks projects in a simple spreadsheet
- Spends 2+ hours/day juggling apps

**Pain Points:**
- Forgets to follow up with leads
- Misses messages in Instagram DMs
- Manual invoicing is time-consuming
- No centralized view of client conversations

**VERIQ Use:**
- Unified inbox to see all client messages
- AI auto-responds to common questions (pricing, availability)
- AI drafts and sends invoices
- Books consultations automatically

## Persona 4: SME Manager David

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Age** | 45 |
| **Role** | Operations Manager at a 50-person logistics company |
| **Location** | Cape Town, South Africa |
| **Tech Level** | Low (prefers simple, minimal tools) |
| **Income** | $100,000+ / year (company revenue $5M+) |

**Behavior:**
- Uses WhatsApp for customer dispatch, email for formal comms
- Has a part-time IT person who set up Zoho CRM
- Runs operations from a mix of spreadsheets and WhatsApp groups

**Pain Points:**
- Current software stack is too complex
- Employees bypass official tools and use WhatsApp directly
- No visibility into customer interactions

- AI handles routine dispatch questions
- Dashboard shows all customer interactions in real-time
- No training required — works like WhatsApp but better

## Persona 5: Enterprise Director Amara

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Age** | 52 |
| **Role** | Director of Operations at a 500-person retail chain |
| **Location** | Johannesburg, South Africa |
| **Tech Level** | Low (relies on IT team for technical decisions) |
| **Income** | $10M+ / year (company revenue) |

**Behavior:**
- Oversees customer service, sales ops, and logistics
- Currently uses Salesforce (hated by the team), Zendesk, and spreadsheets
- IT team manages integrations and compliance
- Reports to board on customer satisfaction and operational KPIs

**Pain Points:**
- Salesforce is expensive and hated by the team
- Low adoption of current CRM tools
- No single source of truth for customer data
- Compliance and security requirements limit tool choices

**VERIQ Use:**
- Enterprise deployment with SSO and RBAC
- AI agents handle Tier 1 customer support across 50+ stores
- Unified dashboard for board-level reporting
- SOC 2 compliance and data residency controls
- Team actually uses it because it feels like chat, not CRM
