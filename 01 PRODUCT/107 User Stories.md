# User Stories — Phase 1

## Purpose
Define the specific user needs that drive Phase 1 development.

---

## Authentication & Onboarding

- **As a** new user, **I want** to sign up with my email and password **so that** I can create my VERIQ account quickly.
- **As a** new user, **I want** to create my workspace during onboarding **so that** I can set up my business profile.
- **As a** returning user, **I want** to log in with my email and password **so that** I can access my workspace.
- **As a** user, **I want** to reset my password via email **so that** I can regain access if I forget my credentials.

## Integration Setup

- **As a** business owner, **I want** to connect my WhatsApp Business account **so that** I can see customer messages from WhatsApp in VERIQ.
- **As a** business owner, **I want** to connect my Instagram business account **so that** I can see Instagram DMs in VERIQ.
- **As a** business owner, **I want** to connect my Facebook Page **so that** I can see Facebook messages in VERIQ.
- **As a** business owner, **I want** to connect my Telegram bot **so that** I can see Telegram messages in VERIQ.
- **As a** business owner, **I want** to connect my business email **so that** I can see customer emails in VERIQ.
- **As a** business owner, **I want** to embed a chat widget on my website **so that** website visitors can reach me through VERIQ.

## Unified Inbox

- **As a** business owner, **I want** to see all customer messages in one timeline **so that** I never miss a message across channels.
- **As a** business owner, **I want** to see which channel a message came from **so that** I can tailor my response appropriately.
- **As a** business owner, **I want** to see which conversations are unread **so that** I can prioritize my responses.
- **As a** business owner, **I want** to search my conversations **so that** I can find past messages quickly.
- **As a** business owner, **I want** to filter conversations by channel **so that** I can focus on messages from a specific platform.
- **As a** team member, **I want** conversations assigned to me **so that** I know which conversations I'm responsible for.

## AI Reply Suggestions

- **As a** business owner, **I want** VERIQ to suggest replies based on conversation context **so that** I can respond faster.
- **As a** business owner, **I want** to edit AI suggestions before sending **so that** I maintain my brand voice.
- **As a** business owner, **I want** AI to detect customer intent **so that** I can prioritize leads over general inquiries.

## AI Lead Qualification

- **As a** business owner, **I want** AI to automatically qualify leads based on chat **so that** I can focus on high-intent customers.
- **As a** business owner, **I want** to see a summary of qualified leads **so that** I can follow up proactively.

## AI Appointment Booking

- **As a** business owner, **I want** AI to detect when a customer wants to book a meeting **so that** I can offer time slots automatically.
- **As a** customer, **I want** to select a time slot from AI-suggested options **so that** I can book a meeting without back-and-forth.
- **As a** customer, **I want** to receive a calendar invite after booking **so that** I don't forget the appointment.
