# Values

## Purpose
The principles that guide every decision at VERIQ.

## 1. Customer Obsession
Every feature, every line of code, every decision starts with the customer. We don't ask "what can we build?" We ask "what does the customer need?" We measure success not by revenue alone, but by the problems we solve for the businesses that trust us.

## 2. Ship Quality
Speed matters. Quality matters more. We ship fast, but we never ship broken. Quality is not a trade-off; it is a non-negotiable. Every release should make our customers' lives better, not introduce new problems.

## 3. Think Long-Term
We optimize for the decade, not the quarter. Short-term gains never justify long-term damage. We invest in infrastructure, relationships, and architecture that compound over time. We are building a 100-year company.

## 4. Own Outcomes
Ownership is not about control; it is about accountability. Every team member acts like an owner. We don't pass blame. We don't wait for permission. We identify problems and we fix them. We ship.

## 5. Default Transparent
Information flows freely at VERIQ. We default to open: open code reviews, open roadmaps, open post-mortems, open communication. Trust is built through transparency. When things go wrong, we share. When things go right, we celebrate.

## 6. Stay Lean
Resources are finite. Focus is infinite. We stay lean not to cut costs, but to force clarity. Constraints breed creativity. We do fewer things better. We say no more than we say yes.
