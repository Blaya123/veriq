# Brand Guidelines

## Purpose
Define how VERIQ looks, sounds, and feels to the world.

## Voice & Tone

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Confident | We know what we're building and why. No hedging, no apologies. |
| Warm | We speak to humans, not businesses. Empathy over formality. |
| Clear | Jargon-free. Simple words. Direct messages. No fluff. |

## Visual Identity

### Logo Usage
- Primary logo: VERIQ wordmark in brand gradient
- Icon: Abstract "V" mark representing conversation flow and connection
- Minimum clear space: 1x the height of the "V" on all sides
- Never stretch, rotate, or recolor the logo
- Dark mode: white logo on dark backgrounds
- Light mode: dark logo on light backgrounds

### Typography

| Type | Font | Weight | Usage |
|------|------|--------|-------|
| Display | Inter | 700 | Headlines, hero text |
| Heading | Inter | 600 | Section headings |
| Body | Inter | 400 | Paragraphs, content |
| Mono | JetBrains Mono | 400 | Code, data, numbers |

### Imagery Style
- Clean, minimal photography with ample negative space
- Illustrations use the brand palette with flat, modern style
- Screenshots show real VERIQ interfaces, not mockups
- People photography: genuine, diverse, working in real environments
